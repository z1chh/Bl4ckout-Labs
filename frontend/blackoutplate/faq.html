PRODUCT INFORMATION

Our display technology incorporates a unique material embedded within glass to enhance durability. The BL4CKOUT Plate utilizes the latest technology that allows you to seamlessly switch between displaying your license plate and hiding it, all through a remote control.


We have meticulously engineered our product to stand the test of time and endure a variety of environmental conditions. Each unit is constructed with resilience in mind, ensuring that it remains fully operational and visually impeccable regardless of weather extremes, be it intense sunlight, heavy rain, or freezing temperatures. This robustness extends not only to the physical durability of the display but also to its continued performance, ensuring clarity, visibility, and reliability.


The BL4CKOUT Plate is designed with an innovative mechanism that allows it to retract and expose the license plate fully, ensuring the vehicle remains compliant with parking regulations when stationary. Our product's transparency when deactivated means it won't drain your car's battery, offering an efficient solution without compromising on legal requirements or energy consumption.


Our product lineup is designed to ensure universal compatibility across a wide range of vehicle types in North America, fitting all standard car license plates, including specific dimensions for US motorcycles, as well as accommodating EU car license plate standards. This inclusivity covers North American car plates at 12 inches by 6 inches, motorcycles at 6 inches by 3 inches, and the European standard car plates at 52cm by 11cm, providing a versatile solution for drivers looking to maintain privacy while ensuring compliance with local regulations.


The device is designed for use on private property or at car shows for display purposes. We advise checking with local laws before use on public roads, as regulations vary by location. We explicitly prohibit its illegal use and emphasize that it is the user's responsibility to comply with local laws and regulations.


The BL4CKOUT Plate comes with a remote control, giving users the ability to conceal or reveal their license plate instantly. Additionally, the device features an indicator light, ensuring users can easily determine its operational status at a glance.


Our product is backed by a comprehensive 30-day warranty, which safeguards against any defects in materials and craftsmanship, ensuring our customers receive only the highest quality.
