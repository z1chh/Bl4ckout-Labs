The BL4CKOUT Plate utilizes the latest technology that allows you to seamlessly switch between displaying your license plate and hiding it, all through a single click of a remote control.


We have meticulously engineered our product to stand the test of time and endure a variety of environmental conditions. Each unit is constructed with resilience in mind, ensuring that it remains fully operational and visually impeccable regardless of weather extremes, be it intense sunlight, heavy rain, or freezing temperatures.

Through extensive testing, we have confirmed that our product is reliably operational in temperatures ranging from -30 °C to 70°C. Furthermore, it has achieved an IP65 rating, signifying a high level of protection against dust ingress and low-pressure water jets from any direction.


The BL4CKOUT Plate is designed with an innovative mechanism that allows it to retract its opacity and expose the license plate fully, ensuring the vehicle remains compliant with parking regulations when stationary. Our product lineup is designed to ensure universal compatibility across a wide range of vehicle types in North America and EU, fitting all standard car license plates, including specific dimensions for US motorcycles, as well as accommodating EU car license plate standards.

This inclusivity covers:

  • - ALL Standard North American car plates: 12" x 6" (30 cm x 15 cm)
  • - Motorcycle plates: 7" x 4" (18 cm x 10 cm)
  • - European car plates: 20.5" x 4.3" (52 cm x 11 cm)
