Our display technology incorporates a unique material embedded within glass to enhance durability and functionality. The BL4CKOUT Plate utilizes the latest technology that allows you to seamlessly switch between displaying your license plate and hiding it, all through a single click of a remote control.


We have meticulously engineered our product to stand the test of time and endure a variety of environmental conditions. Each unit is constructed with resilience in mind, ensuring that it remains fully operational and visually impeccable regardless of weather extremes, be it intense sunlight, heavy rain, or freezing temperatures.

Through extensive testing, we have confirmed that our product is reliably operational in temperatures ranging from -30 °C to 70°C. Furthermore, it has achieved an IP65 rating, signifying a high level of protection against dust ingress and low-pressure water jets from any direction.


The BL4CKOUT Plate is designed with an innovative mechanism that allows it to retract its opacity and expose the license plate fully, ensuring the vehicle remains compliant with parking regulations when stationary. Our product's transparency when deactivated means it won't drain your car's battery, offering an efficient solution without compromising on legal requirements or energy consumption.


Our product lineup is designed to ensure universal compatibility across a wide range of vehicle types in America and EU, fitting all standard car license plates, including specific dimensions for US motorcycles, as well as accommodating EU car license plate standards.

We currently offer 3 different sizes:

  • - North America plates: 12" x 6" (30 cm x 15 cm)
  • - Motorcycle plates: 7" x 4" (18 cm x 10 cm)
  • - European car plates: 20.5" x 4.3" (52 cm x 11 cm)


Our product is compatible with, but not restricted to those countries:
  • - America : United States, Canada, Mexico, Costa Rica, Panama, Honduras, Guatemala, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Colombia, Ecuador, Brazil, etc.
  • - Asia : Philippines
  • - Europe ( All European Union Countries ): Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Poland, Norway, etc.


Clients are responsible for verifying that their license plate correctly fits our bracket, even if their country is listed in our compatibility list. It is essential that you measure your plate's dimensions carefully and compare them with our bracket specifications to ensure a proper fit.
