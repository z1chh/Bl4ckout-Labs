ABOUT US

BL4CKOUT Labs embodies the spirit of our childhood dreams—transforming passion into innovations.

Welcome to BL4CKOUT Labs—founded by a close group of friends united by a lifelong passion for cars and motorcycles. Growing up, we watched our dreams fade into the background as reality took the forefront. BL4CKOUT Labs is a place where dreams come true. BL4CKOUT Labs is where we decided to stop dreaming and start doing.

Fueled by a blend of nostalgia and a drive for innovation, we've poured months of hard work into creating mods that capture the essence of creativity and individuality. Our flagship product, the BL4CKOUT Plate, is the pinnacle of our efforts — slim, sleek, and perfectly understated. It’s not just a license plate cover; it’s a declaration of style and discretion.

As we continue our journey, our mission remains clear: to chase the dreams of our youth and empower others to do the same. We believe in the power of action — of taking steps every day towards making your visions a reality. If you’ve had dreams you let fade away, we hope that we can inspire you to chase your dreams and make them a reality.

It's the relentless pursuit of that perfect synergy between aesthetics and functionality that fuels our days and nights. At the heart of our creations is a passion for excellence and a dedication to the enthusiasts' spirit.


Our product was conceived from a desire to blend sophistication with a touch of mystery. For those who see their vehicle as an extension of their persona, our plate offers a canvas for identity and a platform for discretion.


Our ideas take shape in a hive of creativity, buzzing with ingenuity. Every product is a child of ambition, designed in our labs where innovation meets meticulous craftsmanship.


Quality is our creed, and it's etched into every BL4CKOUT Plate we craft. We commit to the highest standards, from selecting premium materials to conducting exhaustive tests, ensuring that your plate is not just a product but a proud emblem of durability and finesse.


Indeed, individuality is key. We offer bespoke personalization services that allow you to imprint your personal style onto your BL4CKOUT Plate, because we believe every journey should be signature, every mile a statement.


We're not just chasing the future; we're driving it. Our vision is a world where every ride is empowered with customizable tech, and every driver is an artist of their own roadworthy masterpiece.
