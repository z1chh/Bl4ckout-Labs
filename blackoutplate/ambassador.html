Acceptance into our Ambassador Program does not guarantee a free product. Based on our evaluation of each application, selected ambassadors may either receive a product for free or obtain a minimum discount of 10%. Regardless of the outcome, every ambassador is provided with a personalized discount code that offers 10% off to their followers. At BL4CKOUT Labs, we believe in giving everyone a chance. Make us proud!

In our evaluation process, we carefully assess the number of followers, views on posts, and overall engagement. Ambassadors with at least 50,000 followers have the potential to receive a product for free, although this does not automatically guarantee a free BL4CKOUT plate. Each application is considered on its individual merits, with an emphasis on engagement and influence within your community.

Payments are issued biweekly via PayPal, based on the commissions earned through the sales generated by your custom discount code. Each ambassador starts with a commission rate of 10% of the profits generated, with potential increases depending on sales performance.

At BL4CKOUT, we are driven by a passion for automotive innovation and expression. We empower car and motorcycle enthusiasts to explore creativity in vehicle modification, embrace the thrill of motorsports, and uphold the freedom of personal expression. Our ambassadors are vital in representing these core values and inspiring their audiences.

Applications are typically processed within 7 business days. However, during peak times, the review process may take longer. We appreciate your patience and enthusiasm for joining our community.

All applicants will need to place an order (starting with a 10% discount) in order to receive a personalized discount code. Note that the discount code will be emailed to you upon reception of your order number.

Apart from earning commissions, our ambassadors gain early access to new product launches and can be featured on our social media platforms. We are constantly innovating, and top performers in our program will have the exclusive opportunity to experience and promote groundbreaking products in the car modification industry.

Contact us at support@bl4ckoutlabs.com for more information.
